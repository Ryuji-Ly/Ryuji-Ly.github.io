Elian Grey - Victor Ashford

Titles

  • Anchor of Misfortune
  • Reincarnator
  • Worn Voyager
  • Savior

Summary

{Fantasy Life}, a game said to be impossible. Elian reincarnated in the world of this game he had only heard about, reborn as Victor Ashford, an unknown character of the game.

He is ordinary, with no talent for magic or martial arts. Instead, the sole grace Elian has in this world is the ability to retry, endlessly.

Victor Ashford. The Misfortune Anchor. He hoards the despair and suffering of the world. Among all the various timelines, his mere existence allows for a more peaceful future unlike those he did not reincarnate in.
