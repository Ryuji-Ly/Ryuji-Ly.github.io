Lexaurum - Golden Dragon of Merit

Titles

  • Law Encoder
  • Realm Watcher
  • Ascendant

Summary

Lexaurum, the Golden Dragon of Merit, is a magnificent being tasked with encoding the very laws of reality. As a Realm Watcher, Lexaurum oversees the myriad realms, ensuring the stability and order of their existence. Contrary to popular belief, Lexaurum is not from an ancient, affluent lineage. Instead, they lead a modest middle-class lifestyle, constantly overworked and dedicated to their duty

Lexaurum's life involves endless travel across the universe, maintaining balance and harmony. Despite their grandiose title and the misconceptions surrounding their origin, Lexaurum's existence is one of relentless responsibility. They often find themselves exasperated with one particular world full of moronic inhabitants, adding a touch of irony to their otherwise noble role.
