Balaram - Mana Lord

Titles

  • Silver Guardian
  • Mana Lord
  • Authority Holder of Energy
  • The Arcanist
  • Knowledge Giver
  • Direct Creation of Deus
  • Transcendent

Summary

Based on the template of the human Balar Kendrakes, a scholar. Once he became an Absolute Transcendent, he grew bored, unable to find joy as he had all the knowledge he could ever want right at his fingertips. Hence, he connected with the Time Stream until the end of Time, never returning to the normal time flow ever again.

He directly dispersed his body into pure energy and scattered it into countless worlds, bringing or empowering miracles and magic in those worlds.
