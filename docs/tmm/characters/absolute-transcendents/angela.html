Angela - Life Sovereign

Titles

  • Silver Guardian
  • Life Sovereign
  • Authority Holder of Life
  • True Immortal
  • The Undying
  • Carer of the Myriad Multiverses
  • Direct Creation of Deus
  • Transcendent

Summary

Based on the template of the human Hyuma Mercel. She is known to be the weakest of the Silver Guardians, in terms of lethality, the weakest of the Ten Guardians. However, she is the one Absolute none would want to fight, not even God Reaper. As the Absolute of Life, she is even amongst Transcendents, unkillable. Her ability to survive makes it so she can exist even without her Source. So long the Origin Concept of Life exists, she is eternal.

As the Life Sovereign, all those that are considered Living, fall under her domain of command, this includes all forms of undeath, ghosts, souls and the like.

After splitting up countless lesser clones to look after all beings, she fell into a deep slumber, only waking up once called upon by her fellow Absolute Transcendents.
