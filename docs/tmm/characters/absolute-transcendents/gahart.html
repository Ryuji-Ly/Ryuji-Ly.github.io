Gahart - Guardian of Space

Titles

  • Great Guardian
  • Guardian of Space
  • Authority Holder of Space
  • Master of Dimensions
  • Wall of Lesser Nothing
  • Direct Creation of Deus
  • Transcendent

Summary

Gahart is responsible for the creation of new Multiverses when old ones collapse and die. He maintains the barriers between dimensions as well as keeps the space stable. He and The Guardian of Void work together and take the largest part in holding off the erosion of Nothingness. He is also responsible for the solidification of the multiversal barriers after Forgotten Grand War.

The Guardian of Space resides within countless space folds, impossible to find or break into by most.
