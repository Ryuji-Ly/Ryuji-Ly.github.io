Summary

The God Reaper, based on the template of the skeletal remains of the human Pontin Regislat. The Kindest King. His commitment to remain benevolent even when the entire nobility class turned against him earned Deus' respect, who used the broken remains of God Reaper to create the last of the Silver Guardians. Deus taught God Reaper everything he knew, and while God Reaper could only retain mere fragments of the knowledge, it already set him above all else.

Back when Deus governed the Deus Empire and The Guardian lead the Silver Guardians, God Reaper was both the ultimate weapon as well as the greatest mercy for those who opposed Deus' rule.

As an Absolute Transcendent, God Reaper merely kept the Origin Concept of Death close, while he let nature take its course for the various worlds.
