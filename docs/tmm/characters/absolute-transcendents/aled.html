Summary

Based on the template of the human Merin Alederan, Aled is designed with the talent and physique capable of mastering all sword arts. His nickname Aled of Blood comes from a War of Divinity, having turned the surface of a megaplanet into an ocean of blood.

He enjoys sealing his memories and strength to wander different universes and entertain various challenges and battles.
