Angemartin - Warden of Time

Titles

  • Great Guardian
  • Warden of Time
  • Authority Holder of Time
  • Creator of the Time Stream
  • Time Master
  • The Predator of Time
  • Direct Creation of Deus
  • Transcendent

Summary

Angemartin, like most other Absolute Transcendents, is in deep sleep while lesser clones take care of trivial matters that do not require the attention of an Absolute Transcendent. He is the creator of the Time Stream which many Transcendents use, making it one of the trademark abilities of a Transcendent.

His clones hunts down various redundant Time Axes to prevent overflows happening on Multiversal scale.

The Warden of Time is the only Absolute who used his Authority to manifest an Origin Concept into physical form and make its influence accessible to others. The Time Stream is heavily guarded, as its destruction can heavily damage the integrity of Time in The Myriad Multiverses.
