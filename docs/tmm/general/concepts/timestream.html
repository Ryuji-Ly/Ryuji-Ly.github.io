The Time Stream

The Time Stream is a creation by Angemartin, the Warden of Time. It is the physical form of the Origin Concept of Time and is the highest order of control matrixes of Time.

It is used as a tool by almost all Absolute Transcendents to travel around the Myriad Multiverses or record anomalies.

Though not meant for it, the Time Stream is one of the reasons Transcendents are far above all non-Transcendents as it allows them a form of Omniscience, as well as further empower their functional Immortality.
